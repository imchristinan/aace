ABOUT

Xin Chow is an app for Asian-Americans made by Asian-Americans. Growing up, we came to know feelings of confusion and displacement all too well while feeling torn between our two identities of being an Asian raised in America. Through a desire to grow closer to our cultural roots, Xin Chow aims to bridge that gap and create a space for Asian-Americans to share their experiences and learn more about their culture, one recipe at a time.
